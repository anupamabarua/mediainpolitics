The Modern Campaign Playbook

Modern campaigns are focused on targeting specific voters instead of speaking to everybody. They use microtargeting, voter registration, and digital ads to find new supporters and current supporters. Social media is now a basic component of a campaign. Social media is a fast way to reach out to younger voters asking their support.
