Opinion Media

Beyond the nightly news — the world of cable news, political podcasts, and partisan media that shapes how millions of Americans understand politics.

What Is Opinion Media?

There's an important difference between news reporting (journalists gathering facts about events) and opinion media (commentators analyzing, criticizing, or advocating). Much of cable news and political podcasting is primarily opinion — but it's often packaged to feel like straight news. Understanding this distinction is essential for being an informed media consumer.

Major Cable & Online News Networks
RIGHT-LEANING

Fox News

📺 Cable TV · Founded 1996

The highest-rated cable news network in the U.S., Fox News is known for its conservative commentary and prime-time opinion hosts. While it has a news division with traditional reporting, its most-watched shows (Hannity, Tucker, Ingraham) are explicitly opinion programming. Its framing of issues consistently favors Republican positions.

Avg. viewer: 65+, predominantly Republican, Southern and rural areas
LEFT-LEANING

MSNBC

📺 Cable TV · Founded 1996

Created the same year as Fox News, MSNBC evolved from a general news channel into a left-leaning opinion network, especially in primetime. Rachel Maddow, Joy Reid, and others offer heavily liberal analysis. Like Fox, MSNBC has straight news programming, but its identity is built on political commentary that favors Democratic positions.

Avg. viewer: 55+, predominantly Democratic, urban and suburban
CENTER / VARYING

CNN

📺 Cable TV · Founded 1980

The original 24-hour news network, CNN has attempted to position itself as more centrist than Fox or MSNBC, though critics from both sides argue otherwise. Has undergone major changes in recent years trying to reach a broader audience. Strong on breaking news and investigative journalism, weaker on opinion leadership.

Broad demographic, airports, hotels — widely viewed internationally
ALTERNATIVE RIGHT

Breitbart

🌐 Online News · Founded 2007

Breitbart is a far-right online news site known for nationalist, anti-immigration, and anti-establishment content. Under Steve Bannon, it became a major force in conservative media and helped shape the ideological ground for Trump's 2016 campaign. Mainstream media organizations generally do not consider it a credible news source.

Far-right audience, strong overlap with MAGA movement
ALTERNATIVE LEFT

The Young Turks

🌐 Online / YouTube · Founded 2002

One of the first major online political news and commentary channels, TYT offers left-progressive analysis. Known for being highly critical of both Republicans and centrist Democrats. A major force in shaping online progressive media culture and one of the first political channels to find a massive audience on YouTube.

Progressive youth audience, online-first, under 40
OPINION / SATIRE

The Daily Show

📺 Comedy TV / Online · Founded 1996

Satirical news program that has been a major cultural force in political media. Hosted by Jon Stewart (and later Trevor Noah, now back to Stewart), it shaped how a generation of young Americans consumed political news — through comedy and irony. Research shows its viewers often tested more politically informed than those of straight news.

Young adults, college-educated, left-leaning
The Podcast Revolution

Political podcasts have become a dominant force in political information — especially for under-50 voters. Here's a look at the major players across the spectrum.

🎙️

The Joe Rogan Experience

Host: Joe Rogan · 30M+ listeners per episode

The most downloaded podcast in the world. Rogan covers politics, culture, comedy, science, and MMA in multi-hour conversations. Difficult to categorize politically — he has hosted both Bernie Sanders and Donald Trump, and endorsed Bernie Sanders, Tulsi Gabbard, and Trump at different times. His massive, mostly-male audience makes him a critical media force in elections.

LIBERTARIAN / CENTRIST / VARYING
🎙️

Pod Save America

Hosts: Former Obama staffers Jon Favreau, Tommy Vietor, Jon Lovett · 3M+ weekly

Founded by Obama White House alumni, Pod Save America is the flagship liberal political podcast. Known for sharp political analysis, news breakdowns, and progressive advocacy. A major fundraising and voter registration tool during elections. Represents a new model of media: campaign insiders turned independent commentators.

LEFT-LEANING
🎙️

The Ben Shapiro Show

Host: Ben Shapiro · Daily Wire · 2M+ daily downloads

One of the most downloaded conservative podcasts. Shapiro offers rapid-fire conservative commentary, debates, and news analysis. Known for the phrase "facts don't care about your feelings." The Daily Wire, which he co-founded, has become a major conservative media empire, expanding into film and streaming content.

CONSERVATIVE
🎙️

The Daily (New York Times)

Host: Michael Barbaro · 4M+ daily downloads

The New York Times' flagship daily podcast, widely considered one of the best journalism podcasts in the world. Long-form reported stories on the day's biggest news events. One of the highest-quality examples of audio journalism — sourced from the Times' reporting with added depth and context. Consistent top charts placement since 2017.

CENTER-LEFT / JOURNALISM
