Why It Matters

Everyday people interact with all sorts of media. In politics, the media is often considered the “fourth estate" of the government. Media is important in politics because it “educates, informs and entertains” voters. In American politics, the media shapes public opinion by spreading candidates' messages through campaigns. These campaigns are spread through social media, TV, podcasts, and online news.

This site digs deeper into the roles of media in poltics.
