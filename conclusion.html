Why You Should Care about how media impacts poltics?

Democracy runs on the public being educated. They are educated through the media as it informs and entertains them. The media plays a vital role in shaping the public agenda and forming public opinion. It creates a space for people to discuss, debate, and share their opinions.

Tips on how to consume media better
01

Check Your Sources

Use fact-checking sites like PolitiFact, FactCheck.org, and Snopes before believing or sharing something.

02

Diversify Your Feed

Follow some credible sources that cover politics from different angles so you don’t only see one side.

03

Notice Emotional Triggers

If a post makes you instantly angry or excited, pause before sharing it because there might be something sketchy about it.

04

Separate News from Opinion

Figure out whether something is reporting facts or just giving an opinion.

05

Avoid Bias within Yourself

We all have confirmation bias so challenge yourself to read a story from a different perspective.

06

Think Critically

Ask who made it, why they made it, and what evidence they used.
